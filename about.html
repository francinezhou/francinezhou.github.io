Francine Zhou

(she/her)

is a visual artist based in New York and Shanghai.
Her practice reflects on the subtle relationships between human memory and urban landscape.
Through digital media, painting, photography and calligraphy, her work captures momentary sentiments as individuals interact with nature in a constructed environment.
She also offers on-site lettering and calligraphy for live events.
She is currently pursuing a BFA in Communication Design and a Minor in Photography at Parsons School of Design.
